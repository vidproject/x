Tips

  • Every tracked account loads automatically into a single unified view — there's no per-account download to pick. Accounts in the header lists what's loaded and lets you click a handle to filter the table to just that account.
  • ? in the search box matches any single character; * matches any run.
  • Click ▾ on any column header to filter by that column's unique values, Excel-style.
  • Click any row to open the full tweet detail panel on the right.
  • Use Columns to toggle visibility — including Deletion detected, which is off by default.
  • The URL updates as you filter; bookmark or share it to come back to the same view.
  • This is a public archive. Captures mirror what X served the browser at capture time — no editorial filter.
